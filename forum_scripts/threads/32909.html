Well check the servlet specs I would say ...

In your web.xml you can define error pages which showup when for instance a HTTP 500 would occur (which is what is happening in your case). You can then define a URL/page to go to.

Code:
<!--  Error pages -->
<error-page>
	<error-code>404</error-code>
	<location>/error-pages/error-404.jsp</location>
</error-page>
<error-page>
	<error-code>500</error-code>
	<location>/error-pages/error-500.jsp</location>
</error-page>
