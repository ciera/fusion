Error 500 is not related; it looks like you may be attempting to use JSP 2.0 expressions when running JSP 1.2. The Spring Web Flow samples use JSP 2.0 for elegance.

What version of Doug Lea's util.concurrent jar are you using? We tested against 1.3.4. The class that queried to determine if it is in the classpath is:

Code:
EDU.oswego.cs.dl.util.concurrent.ReentrantLock;
Double check; make sure that class and the rest of util.concurrent is in your classpath.

Just out of curiosity; are you running on JDK 1.4 or JDK 1.3?
