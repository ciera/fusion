I guess your NPE is caused by these lines

Code:
CabeceraEntradaAlmacen cabeceraEntradaAlmacen = (CabeceraEntradaAlmacen)		
context.getFlashScope().get("cabeceraEntradaAlmacenCommand", CabeceraEntradaAlmacen.class);
System.out.println("Event myLoad(RequestContext context)"+cabeceraEntradaAlmacen.toString());
You are probably trying to access your formObject, judging by the name of the object you are trying to retrieve. To retrieve the formObject. Just call the getFormObject method. Also you are trying to retrieve it from the flashScope while you configured your object to be on the flowScope. However just use the intended methods for that.

Simply change it to

Code:
CabeceraEntradaAlmacen cabeceraEntradaAlmacen = (CabeceraEntradaAlmacen) getFormObject(context);	
System.out.println("Event myLoad(RequestContext context)"+cabeceraEntradaAlmacen.toString());
I suggest you read the Spring Web Flow reference guide and take a look at the javadocs. It might give you some more insight in what is happening, this isn't the first time you are trying to reinvent something which the framework already provides for you .
