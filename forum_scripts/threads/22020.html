Nicolas,

It's clear your configured DefaultBeanValidator's supports(clazz) method is returning false, which is why this exception is being thrown. So basically, your validator is saying it can't validate your kind of form object. Now why it's saying this I don't know, but it looks completely like a validator setup issue, not a SWF issue, and I see nothing preventing integration between commons-validator and SWF...

I'd check the logic in DefaultBeanValidator.supports and see if you can figure out why it's returning false for your ProjectCredential form object.
