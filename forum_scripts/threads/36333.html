When you return your ModelAndView instance from your controller, use the org.springframework.web.servlet.view.RedirectView class as the view.

Code:
return new ModelAndView(new RedirectView("urlToForm.htm"));
Where urlToForm.htm is the url to your form.

Even beter would be to specify it in your configuration. That way your controller can stay free of logic for redirecting or forwarding to a certain view.

The controller simply returns the succesview.

Code:
protected ModelAndView onSubmit(HttpServletRequest request,HttpServletResponse response, Object command,BindException errors) throws Exception {
	UserInfo userInfo = (UserInfo) command;
	userService.createUser(userInfo);
	return new ModelAndView(getSuccesView());
	
}
In your configuration you configure your succes view, notice the redirect: keyword.

Code:
<bean id="yourcontroller" class="com.yourcompany.yourapp.controller.YourController>
    <property name="successView" value="redirect:urlToForm.htm"/>
</bean>
